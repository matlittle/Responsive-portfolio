About Me

Hello world, my name is Mathew Little. I'm currently working as a support technician for a software company, but have grandiose dreams of moving into a development role. Programming and Computer Science have always been a fascination of mine. I'm extremely excited to absorb all the information I can from this course, and hope to gain the knowledge and skill-set needed to propel my career into a production/development position.

Some of my favorite hobbies are video games, watching football, attempting to learn programming languages, and tinkering with all sorts of different mechanical things. I love building and designing things from scratch, and it's part of what interests me so much about web and software development. The idea that I can create whatever I put my mind to has always drawn me to further my education so I can obtain a career that will allow me to do just that.
